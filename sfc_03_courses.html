Flora Identification Techniques

Sarawak is home to one of the world's most diverse rainforest ecosystems, with over 8,000 species of flowering plants documented across its national parks.

Learning Objective: Identify at least 10 protected plant species using visual, tactile, and contextual cues.

1. Visual Identification

  • Leaf shape and arrangement — alternate, opposite, whorled
  • Bark texture and colour — smooth, furrowed, scaly
  • Flower structure — petals, colour patterns, inflorescence
Never touch, pick, or disturb any plant without authorisation. Collection is prohibited under the Wildlife Protection Ordinance 1998.
